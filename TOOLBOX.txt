  2) Les scripts chargent .env automatiquement via scripts/load_env.sh
  3) Fix deps (pydantic): ./scripts/desk_pro_requirements_fix.sh
  4) Sanity: ./scripts/desk_pro_sanity.sh
  5) Test HTTP: ./scripts/desk_pro_http_test.sh
